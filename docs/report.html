01 · The idea

Anthropic showed the cut exists; we run it in reverse

The workspace paper (July 2026) found that a small, privileged subspace in a model's middle layers carries its flexible reasoning. Ablate that J-space and multi-hop reasoning collapses while shallow recall survives untouched. So the two capabilities are causally separable, and reasoning is the small part.

reasonprune inverts the cut: identify the components serving closed-book recall, remove those, and guard everything reasoning depends on. A model that knows less but thinks just as well; facts come from the context window, tools, and the web.

Prior art check (full survey in the repo): calibration-based pruning exists (Wanda, REAP), and a forget/retain ratio exists for small-scale unlearning (Pochinkov & Schoots 2024). Nobody has used the ratio for compression, nobody prunes with a knowledge/reasoning split, and nobody has touched the workspace paper for pruning. Three claims, all still open as of this writing.

02 · Method

Two decks, one wiretap, a guarded cut

STEP 1Two calibration decks K: closed-book trivia (answer lives in the weights). R: reasoning with every fact in the prompt (memory is useless), incl. worked GSM8K solutions so long-form chains are covered.
STEP 2Wiretap every channel Each MLP channel ≈ one key-value memory slot (Geva et al.). Record activation strength × write-vector size on each deck separately. 86k channels on 0.8B, 1.1M on 27B.
STEP 3Ratio + guard Prunability = importance-on-K / importance-on-R. The guard: channels in the top 30% of reasoning importance are untouchable. Proven load-bearing, see 04.
STEP 4Cut, then verify Masking for science (identical math), physical slicing for a real smaller checkpoint. Evals: held-out fact probes, reasoning tasks, TriviaQA, GSM8K, format controls, perplexity.

Design choices that keep the science honest: the reasoning deck is generated programmatically so answers are correct by construction; knowledge probes come from curated fact tables; eval splits are disjoint from calibration; knowledge evals are open recall because the workspace paper shows multiple-choice survives workspace damage and would hide knowledge loss.

03 · Core result

The dissociation is real and only the guarded ratio finds it

table view

confirmed Not format damage

The open-book control asks capital-city questions in the identical format with the fact supplied in the prompt. At diff@10% it holds at 0.87 (from 0.96) while closed-book capital recall falls 0.81 → 0.32 and author recall 0.72 → 0.04. The recall pathway is severed; question-answering is intact. Tool-call JSON: 1.00 at every level.

confirmed The guard is load-bearing

The raw ratio without reasoning protection (diff_noguard) collapses the model by 20% pruning; so does pruning what knowledge needs most (know, the destructive control, ppl → 100k+). Knowledge and reasoning share substrate; the method works because the guard restricts cuts to the separable part. This replicates the entanglement warning in the unlearning literature.

confirmed Arithmetic improved after pruning

Single-step word math rose 0.48 → 0.76 at diff@10%. Removing noisy memorized associations helping reasoning is the published LASER effect (Sharma et al., ICLR'24); we reproduce it with an entirely different selection mechanism.

04 · External validity

Real benchmarks agree, after teaching the guard about chain-of-thought

First pass (v1): TriviaQA halved, exactly as designed, but GSM8K slipped 0.51 → 0.39. Diagnosis: the reasoning deck contained only short answers, so channels carrying long-form working were never protected. v2 adds 150 worked GSM8K training solutions to calibration (eval slice untouched). Result: more knowledge removed and better math retention. The classic low-importance baseline is worse on both axes at the same perplexity cost.

05 · J-space

From inspiration to instrument running

Sections 03–04 use behavioral signals; the workspace paper contributed the hypothesis. The direct integration is now built and running: sketched Jacobian-transport alignment. Each channel writes a vector into the residual stream; the lens transport Jl = E[∂h_final/∂hl] says how much of that write causally reaches the output. Computing Jl exactly is a d×d matrix per layer (Anthropic used a cluster). We only need ‖Jlw‖ for our channel vectors, and a Johnson–Lindenstrauss sketch delivers it for all 86k channels at once: ~64 random probes, one backward pass each, gradients for every layer simultaneously. Twenty minutes on an M5 Max for what naively costs thousands of backprops.

The first profile already reproduces the paper's signature: transport alignment rises through the middle layers, peaks around layer 19 of 24, and falls at the end; the workspace band, recovered on a local hybrid-attention model by an independent estimator.

pending The ablation that answers "is J-space useful for pruning"

diff_jguard protects channels ranked high by either behavioral reasoning importance or J-alignment. It is sweeping now against diff at 10–40% with full evals. If the tradeoff curve improves, the Jacobian lens earns its place in the loop; if not, that's a real negative result and the report will say so.

06 · The honest ledger

What should keep you skeptical

caveat Perplexity rises

24.6 → 31 at the headline config. Some prose-coherence cost is real. Mitigation path: a short LoRA heal on reasoning-style data only (standard after aggressive pruning, not yet run).

caveat 0.8B is fragile past 10–15%

All strategies collapse long-form generation by 20%+ on a sub-1B model; there is little redundancy to spend. The interesting question is whether headroom grows with scale. The 27B baseline (knowledge 0.844, reasoning 0.950, GSM8K 0.89) and its importance scores are computed and committed; its differential scores concentrate in layers 55–59 of 64, matching where ROME/MEMIT locate factual storage. The sweep is parked pending small-model proof.

caveat Memory math, no spin

MLPs are ~⅔ of a dense model. 10% of MLP ≈ 6.5% of total weights: not a product, a measurement. A worthwhile dense win needs 30–50% MLP cuts to hold (plus healing), and the genuinely large prize is MoE experts (~90% of parameters in A3B-class models, REAP shows 50% removable), which is exactly where this differential method should shine. Dense-27B is the hard mode for the approach.

caveat What would falsify this

If reasoning retention at matched knowledge-removal doesn't beat the lowmag control on 2B/4B replications; if the J-guard adds nothing over the behavioral guard; if healed perplexity can't return near baseline; or if the effect shrinks with scale instead of growing. All four are scheduled measurements.
